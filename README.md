## About Me

I'm a 24-year-old third-year Business Analytics and Information Systems student at USF. This GitHub is where I document my journey through AI and agentic development figuring out where the skills take me as I build them.

By day, I work in healthcare administration, where I love digging into the numbers behind why things happen. Outside of work, that same curiosity goes toward soccer analytics — I'm a lifelong Chelsea FC supporter and spend more time than I should looking at xG charts.

My long-term goal is to build a career as a **solopreneur**, turning the development skills I've picked up outside the classroom into real client work. Classes like ISM Business Application Development are where I sharpen the fundamentals; the real-world projects are where I put them to use.
